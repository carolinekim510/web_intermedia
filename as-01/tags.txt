<!DOCTYPE html> - Declation of document type
<html> - Beginning of html
<head> - Header of the page, won't show on  window
<title> - Title of the page, usually shows on tab
<body> - body of the page, anything that are typed in this part will show on window
<br>  - skips the line, line break
<center> - places the elements to center of the page
<h1> - Heading 1 : Font style
